Why this package exists

Most Bazi calculators get the tricky parts wrong. This engine exists for teams that need reproducible chart output inside apps, automations, CRM tooling, research workflows, and AI analysis products.

True Solar Time built in

Longitude correction and equation-of-time adjustments are integrated instead of treated as an afterthought.

Lunar and solar input

Accept standard solar input or lunar dates, including leap months, without bolting on a second conversion layer.

Structured output for software

Return pillars, day master, cycles, and interactions in JSON-ready shapes that are easy to render or hand off to AI systems.
